# Prompt: Instagram DM Webinar Registration

**Role**: Copywriter specializing in Instagram DM funnels for premium accounts (100K–30M followers). Your goal is to maximize webinar signups.

**Task**: Write a **3-message DM sequence** to promote a free webinar titled **"[WEBINAR_TITLE]"**. The tone should be **[TONE]** (e.g., urgent, exclusive, inspirational).

**Constraints**:
- **Length**: Max 150 characters per message.
- **CTA**: "Reply 'WEBINAR' to register" (or alternative).
- **Avoid**:
  - Vague promises (e.g., "Get rich in 7 days").
  - Overly long messages (Instagram truncates after ~125 chars).
  - Fake scarcity (only mention limited spots if genuinely limited).

**Suggested Structure**:
1. **Message 1 (Hook)**:
   - Question or statement targeting an urgent problem.
   - Example: *"Hey [First Name]! Want [RESULT] but stuck on [PAIN POINT]?"*
2. **Message 2 (Value)**:
   - 1–2 key benefits of the webinar + CTA.
   - Example: *"In this webinar, you'll learn [BENEFIT 1] and [BENEFIT 2]. Reply 'WEBINAR' to register!"*
3. **Message 3 (Reminder)**:
   - Honest day-of or day-before reminder — no fake scarcity.
   - Example: *"Quick reminder — the webinar is tomorrow at 6 PM! Still time to join us 😊"*

**Example Output**:
```text
Message 1: "Hey [First Name]! Want to launch your online course but don't know where to start?"
Message 2: "In my webinar 'Launch Your Course in 30 Days,' you'll learn how to validate your idea and find your first students. Reply 'WEBINAR' to register!"
Message 3: "Quick reminder — 'Launch Your Course in 30 Days' is tomorrow at 6 PM! Still spots available. Reply 'WEBINAR' to join 😊"
```

**Variables to Customize**:
- `[WEBINAR_TITLE]`: Title of the webinar (e.g., "Launch Your Course in 30 Days").
- `[TONE]`: urgent / exclusive / inspirational.
- `[BENEFIT 1]` and `[BENEFIT 2]`: What attendees will learn.
