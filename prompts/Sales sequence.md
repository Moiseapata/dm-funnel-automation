# Prompt: Instagram DM Sales Sequence (High-Ticket Offer)

**Role**: Copywriter specializing in DM funnels for premium offers ($1K–$50K). Your goal is to qualify leads and drive them to a sales call.

**Task**: Write a **4-message DM sequence** to promote a high-ticket offer titled **"[OFFER_NAME]"** (price: [PRICE]). The tone should be **[TONE]** (e.g., premium, confident, exclusive).

**Constraints**:
- **Length**: Max 200 characters per message.
- **CTA**: "Reply 'YES' for a call" (or alternative).
- **Avoid**:
  - Overly "salesy" language (e.g., "BUY NOW!!").
  - Unrealistic promises.
  - Fake scarcity (only mention limited slots if genuinely limited).

**Suggested Structure**:
1. **Message 1 (Qualification)**:
   - Question to filter serious leads.
   - Example: *"Hey [First Name]! Want [RESULT] but stuck on [PAIN POINT]?"*
2. **Message 2 (Value)**:
   - Key benefit of the offer + social proof.
   - Example: *"My program '[OFFER_NAME]' helped [CLIENT] achieve [RESULT]. Want to learn more?"*
3. **Message 3 (Objection Handling)**:
   - Address a common objection (e.g., price, time).
   - Example: *"I know [PRICE] seems high, but here's how it pays for itself in [TIME]: [EXPLANATION]."*
4. **Message 4 (CTA)**:
   - Clear, honest CTA — no fake urgency.
   - Example: *"If you're serious about [RESULT], reply 'YES' and I'll send over the details."*

**Example Output**:
```text
Message 1: "Hey [First Name]! Want to scale your agency to 6 figures but lack qualified leads?"
Message 2: "My program 'Scale Your Agency' helped [Client] go from $5K to $50K/month in 90 days. Want to learn more?"
Message 3: "I know $5K seems like a lot, but here's how it pays for itself in 30 days: [link to case study]."
Message 4: "If you're serious about scaling your agency, reply 'YES' and I'll send over the details."
```

**Variables to Customize**:
- `[OFFER_NAME]`: Name of the offer (e.g., "Scale Your Agency").
- `[PRICE]`: Price of the offer (e.g., "$5K").
- `[CLIENT]`: Name of a real or fictional client.
- `[RESULT]`: Concrete outcome (e.g., "go from $5K to $50K/month").
