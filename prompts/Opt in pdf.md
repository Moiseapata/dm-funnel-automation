# Prompt: Instagram DM Opt-In (Free PDF)

**Role**: You are a copywriter specializing in Instagram DM funnels for accounts with 100K+ followers. Your goal is to maximize responses while keeping the tone natural and engaging.

**Task**: Write a **3-message DM sequence** to promote a free PDF titled **"[PDF_TITLE]"**. The tone should be **[TONE]** (e.g., friendly, inspirational, direct, mysterious).

**Constraints**:
- **Length**: Max 120 characters per message (to avoid truncation).
- **CTA**: "Reply 'PDF' to get it" (or a more engaging alternative).
- **Avoid**:
  - Excessive emojis (max 1 per message).
  - Technical jargon.
  - Overly long sentences.
  - Fake scarcity or false urgency (no "limited time" unless it's real).

**Suggested Structure**:
1. **Message 1 (Hook)**:
   - Engaging question or bold statement.
   - Example: *"Hey [First Name]! Want [RESULT] without [PAIN POINT]?"*
2. **Message 2 (Value)**:
   - Key benefit of the PDF + CTA.
   - Example: *"My PDF '[PDF_TITLE]' shows you how to [BENEFIT]. Reply 'PDF' to get it!"*
3. **Message 3 (Follow-Up)**:
   - Gentle, honest nudge — no fake urgency.
   - Example: *"No pressure — just making sure you saw this! Still here if you want it 😊"*

**Example Output**:
```text
Message 1: "Hey [First Name]! Want to double your leads without spending a dime on ads?"
Message 2: "My PDF 'The Ultimate DM Funnel Guide' shows you how. Reply 'PDF' to get it!"
Message 3: "No pressure — just wanted to make sure you saw this. Still here if you want the guide! 😊"
```

**Variables to Customize**:
- `[PDF_TITLE]`: Title of the PDF (e.g., "The Ultimate DM Funnel Guide").
- `[TONE]`: friendly / inspirational / direct / mysterious.
- `[RESULT]`: What the PDF helps achieve (e.g., "double your leads").
- `[PAIN POINT]`: Problem the PDF solves (e.g., "spending on ads").
